### 4. New FAQ Section

**Q: How much should I budget for a new shingle roof in Charlotte in 2026?**
A: For a typical 2,000 SF Charlotte home, budget $14,000-$19,000 for quality architectural shingles installed. Premium options run $19,000-$24,000+.

**Q: Why are Charlotte roofing prices higher than national averages?**
A: Charlotte's strong housing market, strict building codes, and high demand for skilled roofers pushes prices 10-15% above national averages. Our labor costs reflect paying good crews what they're worth.

**Q: Does the shingle color affect the price?**
A: Standard colors are the same price. Designer colors or specialty blends can add $50-$150 per square. In Charlotte, Weathered Wood and Driftwood are most popular - always in stock.

**Q: How do I know if I'm getting a fair shingle price?**
A: Get 3 quotes, compare apples to apples (same shingle brand/line), verify contractor licensing, and never pay more than 30% deposit. If a price is way below others, ask what they're skipping.

**Q: What's the price difference between 3-tab and architectural shingles?**
A: Expect to pay $2.00-$3.00 more per square foot for architectural. Over a 20-square roof, that's $4,000-$6,000 more - but you get 20+ more years of life and better curb appeal.
