# Best Roofing Now SEO + GBP Improvement Plan

Date: 2026-04-28
Site: https://www.bestroofingnow.com
Scope: Ahrefs organic data, DataForSEO keyword/audit data, local SERP review, GBP/directory signals, and existing repo SEO/GBP docs.

## Executive Summary

Best Roofing Now has a strong trust story, broad page coverage, and some meaningful wins, but the site is not yet winning the highest-value Charlotte roofing terms. The opportunity is not "more pages" in general. The highest ROI is to consolidate authority around the core Charlotte money pages, improve GBP/local-pack competitiveness, fix a few technical/schema issues, and build cost/estimate content that matches what currently wins in the SERPs.

Current measurable position:

- Ahrefs reports 34 US organic keywords, 154 estimated monthly organic visits, $25,487 traffic value, and 3 keywords in positions 1-3.
- DataForSEO audit reports a broader footprint: 534 ranked keywords, 12 top-10 rankings, 120 referring domains, 127 striking-distance keywords, and 37,760 striking-distance search volume.
- DataForSEO keyword research found 123 roofing/gutter keywords with measurable Charlotte-area volume, totaling about 6,050 monthly searches.
- GBP/directory signals are solid: visible 5.0 rating with about 62 Google reviews in local listings, BBB A+ accreditation, and consistent NAP: 10130 Mallard Creek Rd Ste 300, Charlotte, NC 28262, (704) 605-6047.

Primary growth constraint:

The site has many pages, but the main commercial terms are still weak: Ahrefs shows the homepage at position 27 for "roofing companies charlotte nc", position 42 for "roof replacement charlotte nc", and no meaningful traffic from "roof repair charlotte nc". Local pack results are also led by competitors, not BRN, for the core queries.

## Priority Plan

### 1. Fix Core Charlotte Money Page Targeting

Goal: Move from broad homepage ranking to dedicated page ranking for the largest Charlotte terms.

Target terms:

- roofing charlotte nc
- roofers charlotte nc
- roofing company charlotte nc
- roof repair charlotte nc
- roof replacement charlotte nc

Actions:

- Make `/roofing-company-charlotte-nc`, `/roofing-charlotte-nc`, `/roof-repair-charlotte-nc`, and `/roof-replacement-charlotte-nc` the primary organic targets, not just the homepage.
- Add stronger visible proof to each: 62+ Google reviews, BBB A+, veteran-owned, 500+ roofs, certifications, service area, recent project photos, and emergency availability.
- Add internal links from homepage, footer, service hub, city pages, blog posts, FAQ pages, and cost pages using exact and partial anchors.
- Make each page answer the SERP's People Also Ask questions in visible HTML and schema.
- Add or verify `ServiceSchema` and `FAQSchema` on roof repair and roof replacement pages.

Success metric:

- Main terms move into top 15 within 60-90 days, then top 10 after link/GBP improvements.

### 2. Upgrade GBP for Local Pack Competitiveness

Goal: Improve visibility for "near me", roof repair, roof replacement, roofing company, and city/service combinations.

GBP actions:

- Verify primary category is `Roofing contractor`.
- Add/verify secondary categories from the GBP setup doc: roof repair shop, gutter cleaning service, gutter service, siding contractor, building restoration service, general contractor, skylight contractor, chimney services, construction company.
- Add all 20+ services from `google-business-profile-setup.md`, especially roof inspection, roof repair, roof replacement, residential roofing, commercial roofing, emergency roof repair, storm damage repair, insurance claim assistance, gutter installation, gutter repair, gutter guards, siding, skylight, roof ventilation, chimney repair, soffit/fascia, flat roof repair, and maintenance.
- Add UTM tracking to the GBP website URL, for example:
  `https://www.bestroofingnow.com/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp`
- Add appointment URL to the free roof inspection or instant estimate flow.
- Add weekly GBP posts for 12 weeks: storm damage, free inspections, roof replacement financing, gutter installation, emergency repair, metal roofing, commercial roofing, Lake Norman service, recent projects.
- Upload 3-5 new photos weekly: team, vehicles, jobsite, before/after, completed roof, gutter work, commercial work.
- Build a review request process that asks for city + service language in the review, for example "roof repair in Huntersville" or "roof replacement in Charlotte".
- Respond to every review with service and location language, without keyword stuffing.

GBP risk/gap:

- I could not verify the live GBP services tab from automation because the repo's GBP Playwright script failed due to missing `playwright`. Treat service/category completion as a manual verification item.

Success metric:

- Local pack appearances for roof repair/replacement/roofing company terms in Charlotte improve within 30-60 days.
- Reviews grow from ~62 to 90+ within 90 days.

### 6. Technical + Schema Cleanup

Goal: Remove issues that dilute crawlers' trust and slow ranking improvements.

Actions:

- Keep the sitemap FAQ inclusion that is already now present.
- Fix dynamic `lastmod` values in sitemap routes so they do not change on every request.
- Audit remaining `cms.bestroofingnow.com` image/video dependencies. If the CMS host is unreliable, move critical media to `/public` or Vercel Blob.
- Resolve Core Web Vitals issues on homepage and priority landing pages. Target LCP under 2.5s and interaction latency under 100ms.
- Verify `ServiceAreaPageSchema` URLs match the real page URLs on top-level service/city pages.
- Ensure review schema is backed by visible review content.
- Add `@graph` JSON-LD over time to connect Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb, Review, and WebPage entities.

Success metric:

- DataForSEO high issues drop from 12 to 0.
- Homepage LCP stays below 2.5s.
