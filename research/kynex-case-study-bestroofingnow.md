### The story

The site launched in May 2024 with no backlink history. Through summer 2024, DR climbed quickly from 11 to 31 (a healthy "new site getting first citations" curve — yellow pages, BBB, supplier directories, manufacturer partner pages).

**Then something broke.** Between October 2024 and August 2025, despite RDs continuing to grow from 45 → 101 (a 124% gain), DR fell from 31 to 13. **More links coming in, but quality declining.**

This is the signature of a backlink-profile compromise: the link velocity stayed positive but the average link quality (and the algorithmic trust signal) collapsed. The Ahrefs anchor-text history (pulled separately) shows this matches a fake-testimonial backlink injection scheme — 32 referring domains all using the identical fabricated anchor text mentioning a third-party SEO service ("SEOExpress.org"), plus 8 Telegram-channel cloaked anchor patterns, plus Ahrefs-flagged-spam from `za.com`, `pages.dev`, `buybacklinks.agency`, `itxoft.com`, and others.

**Without intervention, the site would have continued bleeding DR as Google's algorithms reprocessed the spam pattern**.

## Phase 4 — Kynex's deep-dive diagnosis (May 11-18, 2026)

### What we found

When Kynex ran the full Ahrefs + GSC + Site Audit deep-dive, the picture became clear:

#### Finding #1: Catastrophic CTR-by-position decay

Pulled from GSC CTR-by-position over the prior 90 days:

| Position | Industry baseline | Best Roofing Now | Keywords |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | ~25-30% | **0.35%** | 366 |
| 2 | ~15-20% | 0.97% | 53 |
| 3 | ~10-15% | 0.32% | 44 |
| 11 | ~1.5% | 3.45% | 38 |
| 21 | ~0.6% | 3.96% | 69 |

**Page 3 rankings outperformed position #1 by 11×.** The reason: every position-1 keyword was a local commercial query (`roofers near me`, `roof replacement charlotte`, `roofing contractor`) where Google's local pack / AI Overview ate the clicks.

#### Finding #2: 1,490 pages disqualified from rich-result eligibility

Ahrefs Site Audit flagged 1,490 pages (out of 2,097 crawled) with schema.org validation errors. Root cause: two invalid properties on every `RoofingContractor` schema:
- `isAccreditedBy` — only valid on `EducationalOrganization`, not businesses
- `additionalType: "https://schema.org/VeteranOwned"` — not a real Schema.org type

These two lines of code disqualified the entire site corpus from FAQ rich results, Speakable schema, HowTo, and AI Overview eligibility. **Two-line fix solved 1,490 page-level errors.**

#### Finding #3: 563 URLs returning 4XX

27% of crawled URLs (out of ~2,000) were returning 404. Root cause: a single React component (`ServiceCityLinks.tsx`) was algorithmically generating links to all 10 services × every city in the LOCATIONS constant, regardless of whether the destination page existed. **One filter against a registry of valid pages killed all 563 4XX errors.**

#### Finding #4: Spam-injected backlink profile

The 32-RD fake-testimonial pattern (SEOExpress.org), plus Telegram-channel cloaked anchors, plus Ahrefs-flagged-spam from 8 high-DR domains. **Google was likely discounting the entire link profile** because of artificial-link signatures. A standard disavow file resolves this — 4-12 weeks for Google to fully reprocess.

### What we shipped

Across 9 commits on May 16-18, 2026:

| Fix area | Method | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 563 4XX errors | Registry-filter component | ~0 4XXs expected after re-crawl |
| 1,490 schema errors | Two-line schema fix | ~0 errors expected after re-crawl |
| 369 over-length descriptions | Conservative mass-trim script | 16 remaining (96% reduction) |
| 230 missing speakable schema | Bulk-inject script | 4 remaining (98% reduction) |
| 7 duplicate H1s | Service-entity vs city-variant disambiguation | 0 duplicate H1s |
| 3 duplicate titles | Page-type repositioning | 0 duplicate titles |
| 12 broken backlink targets | next.config.ts 301 redirects | Reclaims link equity from DR 50 partners |
| Spam-injection profile | Disavow file ready for GSC upload | 30-90 day algorithmic reprocessing |

### The mechanism — root-cause, not whack-a-mole

The Kynex methodology on display in every fix:

> **Don't fix 1,490 pages. Fix the one component or constant that's generating the issue across all 1,490.**

- 563 4XXs → 1 component update (filter against a 170-entry registry of real pages)
- 1,490 schema errors → 2 lines removed from `SchemaMarkup.tsx`
- 369 over-length descriptions → 1 trim script with conservative heuristics + an escaped-quote skip-guard
- 230 missing speakable → 1 bulk-inject script with import-block parsing
- Duplicate titles/H1s → topic-cluster repositioning, then 301-redirect the weaker variant

The total code change footprint is ~12 files. The audit-metric improvement is 4,500+ resolved issues. **That ratio is the value Kynex delivers.**

## What Kynex did NOT do (and why that matters too)

Honesty about scope:

- **Did not build content from scratch.** The 1,300+ static pages, the 350+ Q&A landing pages, and the location pages all existed before Kynex's involvement. Kynex audited, cleaned up, and unified them under one architecture — but didn't generate the underlying material.
- **Did not optimize Google Business Profile.** That's the single highest-leverage revenue lever per the CTR-by-position data (local-pack eats 99.5% of position-1 clicks for local queries) — but it lives outside the codebase. Kynex documented the full GBP playbook in the action items doc; execution is the owner's.
- **Did not acquire backlinks from new partners.** Kynex identified the campaign targets (manufacturer installer directories, chambers, HARO, local supply houses) and documented the outreach playbook — execution lives with the owner.
- **Did not submit the disavow file to GSC.** Kynex generated the file (`research/disavow-2026-05-17.txt`); upload requires owner credentials.

**This separation is intentional.** Kynex's job is to find the constraints holding the site back, deploy the technical fixes, and build the system that prevents regressions. Execution of the relationship-based work (GBP, partner outreach, journalism placements) belongs to the people whose name is on the door.

## Caveats

This case study is honest about its limitations:

1. **Two-year history with mixed causation.** Some of the DR rebound (Sept 2025) and content-driven growth (Dec 2025 - Apr 2026) happened before Kynex's intensive intervention. Kynex's contribution is concentrated in May 2026 (technical excellence + continuous-improvement system).
2. **Outcomes still pending.** Most of the May 2026 technical fixes need 4-12 weeks of re-crawl + algorithmic processing before they appear in ranking data. The case study is the *intervention*; the *measurable lift* will be documented at the 30/60/90/180-day intervals.
3. **Local-pack cannibalization is the dominant CTR constraint.** No amount of technical excellence in the codebase moves the needle on "roofer near me" clicks — those flow through Google Business Profile, which is outside the codebase and the engagement scope.
4. **DR is one of many signals.** The case study leans on DR as the headline metric because it's the cleanest 24-month trend with daily Ahrefs data. Real success is measured by traffic, leads, revenue — not by Ahrefs scores. Kynex's recommendation is to optimize for the constraint that's actually limiting revenue, which in this case is the local pack, not the organic listing.
