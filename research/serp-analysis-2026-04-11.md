## Competitive Landscape Summary

The Charlotte roofing SERP landscape is characterized by:

- **Directory dominance** for head terms ("roofing company", "best roofer") - HomeGuide, Expertise, Yelp, Angi control these
- **Cost content wins** for mid-funnel queries - cost guides, calculators, and pricing pages rank well
- **Dedicated service pages win** for specific service queries - emergency, insurance, storm damage
- **Charlotte Ace Roofing** is the most aggressive content competitor, targeting every keyword variation with year-updated blog posts
- **Exact-match domains** still carry weight (charlotteroofing.com, metalroofingcharlotte.net, lknroof.com)
- **BRN's strongest positions** are in specialized services (emergency, financing, insurance) rather than head terms
- **Biggest opportunities** are in cost/pricing content, storm damage, metal roofing, and Lake Norman

BRN currently ranks for 6 of 15 priority keywords (40%). With the recommended content additions, BRN could realistically compete for 12-13 of these 15 keywords within 3-6 months.
